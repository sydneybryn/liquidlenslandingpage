Built for shooting, not scrolling through menus

Every effect renders live in the viewfinder at full frame rate, so you compose the shot you actually want — no guessing how a filter will look after the fact.

Live, not a filter

Full-resolution capture

Photos capture at up to 48MP. Video records with effects baked in — no separate export step.
